AI Ticketing

Turn plain-language messages into resolved work.

Employees describe the problem the way they'd say it out loud. Nevian's assistant reads it, classifies it, sets priority, and either resolves it end-to-end or hands your team a ticket that already carries every piece of context it needs.

Book a Demo See a ticket's journey

From one message to a decision in about a second.

No forms or dropdowns for the employee to guess through. The assistant does the structuring.

Reads the request in plain language

The employee types what's wrong. The assistant interprets intent instead of forcing a category tree on someone who just wants help.

Classifies, prioritizes, and routes

Category, priority, and the right next step are decided up front, with a confidence score, so nothing sits in an unsorted queue.

Checks your knowledge base

Answers follow your company's approved process, not a generic script. If there's a documented fix, the assistant uses it.

Resolves or escalates with context

Routine requests close automatically. Anything that needs a human is escalated with logs and full device context already attached.

Structured automatically

Every message becomes a structured ticket.

The assistant fills in the fields a technician would normally set by hand, and shows its reasoning as a confidence score so your team can trust it or step in.

  • Category and sub-category
  • Priority based on impact and urgency
  • Recommended next step
  • A confidence score you can act on
